Latest Project

APPLICATION FOR TRANSFER OF STUDENTS FROM OTHER FACULTY

This application makes communication between University employees easier. The application's users are members of the commission and members of the marketing department. Marketing services create a new request, enter the student’s data, and finally forward it to the commission that decides about the status of the student and creates the curriculum for that student. The application allows users to view the request's status, which changes depending on the commission's decisions. The technical aspect of this application is automation, providing a good user interface and an overview of all potential students
